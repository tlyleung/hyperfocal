title: Xin Xin Rong He
description: >-
   On the Vesak Day public holiday, I travelled by bumboat to Pulau Ubin, where the annual Tua Pek Kong Festival was underway. In the heat of the midday sun, the Xin Xin Rong He 「新新荣和」 Teochew opera troupe prepared backstage for their performance, continuing a form of <em>wayang</em> that has long been part of temple festivities on the island. In the scorching weather, devotees lit incense and candles, while Tua Pek Kong flags and festival banners marked the village and jetty. It was tradition in motion: a small island community keeping Chinese street opera alive.
